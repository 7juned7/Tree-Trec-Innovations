In India coconuts are mainly produced in the southern states like Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, etc. The harvesting of coconuts is done manually by climbing the trees which is a very labour-intensive process and poses a serious threat to the life of the climber in case of any mishap like falling off from the tree. It is therefore necessary to develop alternate methods of harvesting which reduce the role of humans and use robots which are capable of climbing the trees and harvesting the crop. These methods should also increase the farmers income, reduce harvesting time. The coconuts can be harvested using a semi-autonomous robot.
