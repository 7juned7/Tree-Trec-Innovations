About Us

Tree Trec Innovations is an interdisciplinary research oriented undergraduate team from Delhi Technological University (Formerly Delhi College of Engineering) Founded in 2023 the team has focused on conducting research in the field of autonomous robotic system for application in Agriculture with special emphasis on coconut cultivation. We are a passionate group of individuals driven by innovation and a shared love for technology. Our diverse team brings together experts in mechanical engineering, software development, and electronics, all united by the common goal of pushing the boundaries of robotics. With a strong foundation in STEM fields, we are committed to designing, building, and programming robots that tackle real-world challenges. Our collaborative and hands-on approach fosters creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork among our members. Whether we are participating in competitions, engaging in community outreach, or conducting cutting-edge research, our dedication to robotics knows no bounds. As we embark on this exciting journey, we invite you to join us in shaping the future of agriculture through robotics. Together, we can inspire innovation, foster learning, and make a lasting impact on the world around us.
